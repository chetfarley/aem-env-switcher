Privacy Policy for AEM Environment Switcher

Last Updated: January 2025

The AEM Environment Switcher Chrome extension (“the extension”) is designed solely to help users quickly switch between configured Adobe Experience Manager environment URLs. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains what information is — and is not — collected.

Information the Extension Collects

The extension does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, browsing data, or user-generated content.

The only data used by the extension is:

  • Environment configuration settings (the AEM author and publish URLs you enter yourself).

These settings are stored locally in your browser's chrome.storage.sync area. They never leave your browser and are not transmitted to any server or external service.

No Personal Data Collected

The extension does not collect or process:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Location or IP addresses
  • Authentication information or passwords
  • Financial or payment data
  • Health information
  • Browsing history
  • User activity (mouse, clicks, keystrokes, etc.)
  • Website content

No Data Sharing

The extension does not sell, share, or transfer any data (including configuration settings) to any third party.

No Remote Code

The extension does not load or execute remote code of any kind. All JavaScript, HTML, and CSS files are packaged directly within the extension.

Permissions

The extension uses a small set of browser permissions strictly required for its single purpose:

  • tabs – to read the current tab's URL and open a switched URL in a new tab.
  • storage – to store your AEM environment settings locally.

The extension does not request access to any other data.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not connect to or interact with any third-party servers or APIs.

Changes to This Policy

The privacy policy may be updated in the future. If updated, the revision date at the top of the page will be changed.
